Improving Mental Health Through Gut Wellness: The Hidden Power of Microbes and Probiotics
Roshd Educational Magazine – National Persian Publication (~13 million readers)
Original (Persian title):
«گوارش تا سلامت روان – نقش شگفت‌انگیز میکروب‌ها و پروبیوتیک‌ها»
English Summary:
This article explores the emerging scientific link between gut microbiota and mental health, explaining how microbial balance, probiotics, and diet influence mood regulation, cognitive performance, and stress response. It summarizes recent global research on gut–brain communication and highlights how accessible dietary and microbial interventions may support psychological well-being. Written for a nationwide audience, the article translates complex scientific findings into clear, accessible concepts for students and educators.
